DYNAMIXEL XL330-M288-T WIRING DIAGRAM - 2 MOTORS
Raspberry Pi Pico 2 + SN74HC126N Tri-State Buffer
================================================================================

COMPONENT PINOUTS:
------------------

Raspberry Pi Pico 2:
  GP0  - UART0 TX
  GP1  - UART0 RX  
  GP2  - Direction Control
  3.3V - Power for 74HC126
  GND  - Ground

SN74HC126N (14-pin DIP):
  Pin 1  - 1OE (Output Enable - controls gate 1)
  Pin 2  - 1A  (Input for gate 1)
  Pin 3  - 1Y  (Output from gate 1)
  Pin 7  - GND
  Pin 14 - VCC (3.3V)

DYNAMIXEL XL330 (3-pin JST connector):
  Pin 1 - GND   (Black wire)
  Pin 2 - VDD   (Red wire, 5V)
  Pin 3 - DATA  (Yellow/White wire)


CONNECTIONS:
------------

1. PICO TO SN74HC126:
   Pico GP0 (TX)  ──────────────► 74HC126 Pin 2 (1A)
   Pico GP2 (DIR) ──────────────► 74HC126 Pin 1 (1OE)
   Pico 3.3V      ──────────────► 74HC126 Pin 14 (VCC)
   Pico GND       ──────────────► 74HC126 Pin 7 (GND)

2. SN74HC126 TO DATA BUS:
   74HC126 Pin 3 (1Y) ──────────► DATA BUS (shared line)

3. PICO RX TO DATA BUS:
   Pico GP1 (RX)  ──────────────► DATA BUS (shared line)

4. DATA BUS PULL-UP:
   DATA BUS ───────[ 10kΩ ]─────► 5V Power Rail

5. SERVO #1 (ID=1, LAN Motor):
   Servo1 Pin 1 (GND)  ─────────► Common Ground
   Servo1 Pin 2 (VDD)  ─────────► 5V Power Rail
   Servo1 Pin 3 (DATA) ─────────► DATA BUS (shared line)

6. SERVO #2 (ID=2, AOV Motor):
   Servo2 Pin 1 (GND)  ─────────► Common Ground
   Servo2 Pin 2 (VDD)  ─────────► 5V Power Rail
   Servo2 Pin 3 (DATA) ─────────► DATA BUS (shared line)

7. EXTERNAL 5V POWER SUPPLY:
   5V Supply (+)  ──────────────► 5V Power Rail
   5V Supply (-)  ──────────────► Common Ground

SIGNAL FLOW:
------------

TRANSMIT MODE (GP2 = HIGH):
  Pico GP0 (TX) → 74HC126 Pin 2 → [Buffer ENABLED] → Pin 3 → DATA BUS → Both Servos

RECEIVE MODE (GP2 = LOW):
  Servo responds → DATA BUS → Pico GP1 (RX)
                            → 74HC126 Pin 3 [TRI-STATE/DISABLED]

--------------|-----------------------|------------|------------------

Pico GP1      | DATA BUS             | Yellow     | RX signal

Pico 3.3V     | 74HC126 Pin 14       | Red        | IC power

Servo1 Pin 2  | 5V Rail              | Red        | Servo power
Servo1 Pin 3  | DATA BUS             | Yellow     | Communication
Servo2 Pin 1  | Common Ground        | Black      | Servo ground
Servo2 Pin 2  | 5V Rail              | Red        | Servo power
Servo2 Pin 3  | DATA BUS             | Yellow     | Communication
5V Supply (+) | 5V Rail              | Red        | Power
5V Supply (-) | Common Ground        | Black      | Ground


CRITICAL NOTES:
---------------
1. GP0 connects ONLY to 74HC126 Pin 2 - NOT to ground!
2. Both servos share the same DATA line (daisy-chain)
3. Each servo must have unique ID (1 and 2)
4. 10kΩ pull-up resistor is required on DATA line
5. Common ground is essential for all components
6. 5V supply must provide at least 3-4A for 2 servos
